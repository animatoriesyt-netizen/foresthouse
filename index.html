Our Story

Where the forest
meets the table

Forest House Café is an easygoing retreat tucked inside Pocket C, Sector 17 Dwarka. We built this space for people who want to slow down — to share a meal, breathe a little, and feel genuinely welcomed.

Our menu travels — from thin-crust pizzas to chilli paneer, from strawberry shakes to biryani — but the feeling stays the same: honest, warm, and made with care.

Whether you're catching up over mocktails on the terrace or bringing the family for dinner, this is your table. Stay as long as you like.
